Tour Overview

Where the River Meets the Sea

Head to beautiful Ada Foah at the dramatic meeting point of the Volta River and the Atlantic Ocean. Enjoy a scenic boat ride through the estuary, relax on pristine uncrowded beaches, savour fresh seafood, and experience total coastal relaxation away from the city buzz.

Fun Facts

  1. Ada Foah sits at the stunning point where the mighty Volta River meets the Atlantic Ocean.
  2. Ada is known for some of Ghana's most beautiful, uncrowded, and pristine beaches.
  3. The Ada estuary is a nesting ground for sea turtles and diverse coastal bird species.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

How far is Ada from Accra? ▼
About 2–2.5 hours east of Accra. We travel in a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle with good company along the way.
Is the boat ride included? ▼
Yes. A scenic estuary cruise at the meeting point of the Volta River and the Atlantic Ocean is included in your tour.
Is lunch included? ▼
Yes. Fresh Ada seafood lunch at a beachside spot is included in the price.
Is swimming safe at Ada? ▼
The estuary is calm and ideal for swimming. Ocean conditions vary — your guide will give you the read on the day.
What should I bring? ▼
Swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, sunglasses, and a change of clothes. The beach is sandy and beautiful.
Can I extend and stay overnight? ▼
Yes. Ada has lovely beach resorts. Ask us about overnight arrangements when booking.
