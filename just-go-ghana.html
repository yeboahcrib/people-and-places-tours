Just Go Ghana

8 Days / 7 Nights Departure: Accra, Ghana Activity Level: Moderate Trip Style: Immersive Cultural Adventure
GHANA
Tour Overview

The Motherland Is Calling. Just Go.

Get ready to soak up the vibrant spirit of Ghana! It's not just a destination, it's a soulful experience — and honestly, what is life without experiencing the magic of the Motherland?

Just Go Ghana is a tour designed for those ready to reconnect and rediscover — from solo travelers and couples to families and groups. Immerse yourself in the warmth and electric energy of this incredible West African nation. Over 8 days and 7 nights, you'll explore the deep-rooted culture, the traditions, the people, and the breathtaking places that make Ghana so unforgettable.

Day by Day Itinerary

Your 8-Day Journey

DAY 1 Arrival, Airport Pickup & Welcome Dinner

▼

Upon arrival at Kotoka International Airport, you will be met by a People & Places representative who will assist with your luggage and transfer you to your hotel. After checking in and freshening up, enjoy a welcome dinner at a fine dining restaurant in Accra — a chance to meet your tour guide and prepare for the exciting days ahead.

🍽 Meals included: Dinner

DAY 2 Batik Workshop, Drum Lessons & Accra City Tour

▼

Visit a batik-making center, learn the art of batik and create your own patterns and handmade clothes. Next, head to the Art Center for drum lessons and interaction with local artisans. Afternoon city tour of Accra visiting Kwame Nkrumah Park and Independence Square. Evening free for personal exploration.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch

DAY 3 Aburi Gardens, Cocoa Farm & Waterfalls

▼

Journey into the lush Eastern Region for a day filled with nature and history. Visit Aburi Botanical Gardens, then Tetteh Quarshie's Cocoa Farm — the first cocoa farm in Ghana. Learn about Ghana's cocoa history and taste fresh cocoa beans when available. End at Asenema Waterfalls for photos and relaxation.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch

DAY 4 Ancestral Trip to Cape Coast

▼

A deeply meaningful journey into history and heritage. Visit Assin Manso Ancestral Slave River — a sacred site where enslaved Africans took their last bath on African soil. Then continue to Cape Coast Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Optional evening boat cruise in Elmina. A reflective, educational and transformative day.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch

DAY 5 Free Day

▼

A flexible day designed for rest and personal exploration. Relax and recharge or explore Accra at your own pace. Optional activities include shea butter making, pottery, candle making, beach visits and other cultural experiences. Our team is on standby with recommendations based on your interests.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast

DAY 6 Shai Hills & Akosombo Boat Cruise

▼

Depart for an exciting day of nature and adventure. Explore Shai Hills Resource Reserve on a guided walk, spotting antelopes, baboons and bird species. Continue to Akosombo for a relaxing boat cruise on the Volta River with optional kayaking for the adventurous.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch

DAY 7 Safari Valley Day Experience

▼

Full day at Safari Eco Park — welcomed with freshly squeezed juice, followed by a guided safari tour. Enjoy a farm-to-table meal of your choice. Recreational activities include swimming, horseback riding, animal feeding, golf, cycling, walking trails, indoor and outdoor games, and a children's amusement park.

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast, Lunch

DAY 8 Departure — See You Again!

▼

Time to say goodbye to Ghana. Last-minute shopping or exploration before your transfer to Kotoka International Airport. We'll miss you and hope to see you again soon!

🍽 Meals included: Breakfast

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Can I pay in installments? ▼
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans. After your $400 deposit, you can make monthly payments. Final balance must be cleared 30 days before arrival.
Is my deposit refundable? ▼
The $400 deposit is non-refundable as it immediately reserves accommodations and transport. Any trip cancelled less than 30 days before arrival is 100% non-refundable.
Does the price include flights? ▼
No. You book your own flight to Kotoka International Airport (ACC). We recommend booking early to get the best rates.
Is this good for solo travelers? ▼
Absolutely! Just Go Ghana is designed for solo travelers, couples, families and groups alike. Final price may vary depending on room occupancy selection.
Do I need a visa for Ghana? ▼
Yes, most international travelers require a visa. Visa fees are not included. We recommend applying well in advance through your nearest Ghanaian Embassy.
What vaccinations are required? ▼
A Yellow Fever vaccination card is mandatory for Ghana entry. Consult your doctor regarding Malaria prophylaxis and other standard travel vaccines.
How active is the trip? ▼
Moderate level. Expect walking during city tours, the cocoa farm, and Shai Hills hike. There's plenty of downtime including a designated free day on Day 5.
Is Ghana safe for tourists? ▼
Absolutely. Ghana is widely known as one of the friendliest and safest countries in Africa, especially for the diaspora. Our guides are with you throughout the scheduled itinerary.
