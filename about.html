How It Began

From Passion
to Purpose

People & Places Tours was born out of a simple frustration: too many visitors were experiencing Ghana from the outside — through hotel windows and tourist buses — never getting close to the real heartbeat of this extraordinary nation.

Our founders, local Ghanaians with deep roots across Accra, Cape Coast, and the Volta Region, decided to change that. We built a tour company rooted in community, built on trust, and driven by genuine love for our homeland.

Today, we've welcomed over 1,200 travelers from more than 40 countries — and every single one has left knowing Ghana not just as a destination, but as a home.
